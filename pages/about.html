About

A Tours and Travels Management System is a comprehensive software platform designed to streamline and manage a travel agency's operations. This system encompasses various functionalities, including booking management, itinerary planning, and customer relationship management. Travel agents can efficiently handle bookings for flights, hotels, transportation, and tours, while the system's real-time availability and pricing updates make it easy to provide clients with accurate options. Itinerary planning features enable agents to create custom travel plans, scheduling activities and excursions.
